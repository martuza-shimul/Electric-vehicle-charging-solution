WHY INVEST IN TURKEY

Bridge between Europe, MENA and Asia

Turkey is conveniently located at the intersection of Europe and Asia.

Stable economic growth

Turkey is the 13th largest economy in the world and one of the fastest growing economies among OECD members

Developed infrastructure

Turkey has a well-developed technological infrastructure in transportation, telecommunication and energy

Nature & History

Turkey is home to countless historical heritages and natural attractions.

Culture & Cuisine

Turkish rich cuisine reflects its blended culture derived from various nations with different cultures living together for thousands of years.

Visa-free travel to 110 countries worldwide

Turkish passport holders have visa-free or visa on arrival access to 110 destinations including Singapore, Japan, Qatar and South Korea. Turkish Citizenship offers the easiest and most affordable route into the USA and UK. Turkish citizens may reside in the USA through the USA E-2 Investor Visa (the US E-2 Treaty Program allows citizens from signatory countries including Turkey to reside in the US). Moreover, acquiring a Turkish passport is the first step in relocating to the UK under the Ankara Agreement, allowing Turkish nationals to set up business in the UK and relocate within as little as one month. Coupled with the simplified processing of the Turkish Business Person Visa it is one of the fastest routes to settling in the UK with the whole family.

Investment Options

The most cost-effective option for investors to qualify for the Turkish citizenship program is to buy property in the country for a minimum value of $250,000. The property can be leased to provide rental income. After 3 years from acquiring citizenship investors can re-sell their property. Other alternative investment options include a business set-up as well as cash deposit.

1
$250,000 — Property
  • Residential or commercial property purchase
  • The property can be let for rental income, or sold after 3 years
  • Investors are not limited to investing into only a single property and have the right to acquire several properties
$500,000 — Cash Investment
  • Fixed-asset investment confirmed by the Ministry of Industry and Technology
  • $500,000 deposit in a Turkish bank for a minimum of 3 years
  • Government bonds with the potential to receive interest as income. Investments to be kept for 3 years
